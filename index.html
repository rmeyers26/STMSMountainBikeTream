🏔️ About the Team

Who we are and what we do

The STMS Mountain Bike Team competes in the Arizona Interscholastic Cycling League (AICL), the largest middle and high school mountain bike league in Arizona. Riders develop fitness, bike handling skills, teamwork, and a love for the outdoors while competing on iconic Sonoran Desert trails.

🔗 Arizona Cycling 🏫 STMS School Site
