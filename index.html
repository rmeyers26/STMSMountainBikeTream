🏔️ About the Team

Who we are and what we do

The STMS Mountain Bike Team competes in the Arizona Cycling Association (ACA), the largest middle and high school mountain bike league in Arizona. Riders develop fitness, bike handling skills, teamwork, and a love for the outdoors while competing on iconic Sonoran Desert trails.

📋 How to Join
1

Fill Out Registration

Complete the online registration form with parent/guardian info.

2

ACA Membership

Register on the Arizona Cycling platform.

3

Gear Check

Ensure your bike and helmet meet ACA safety requirements.

4

Attend Practice

Show up to our weekly practices and get ready to race!

🚵 Not Sure Yet? Try It Out — Free!

We also participate in the ACA's Try It Out (free!) program. If you're on the fence, all riders, parents, and sponsors are able to use this one-time program to meet the coaches, meet their fellow riders, and have all questions answered.
