🏔️ About the Team

Who we are and what we do

The STMS Mountain Bike Team Sonoran Trails Middle School and associated with Cactus Shadows Mountain Bike Team competes in the Arizona Cycling Association (ACA), the largest middle and high school mountain bike league in Arizona. Riders develop fitness, bike handling skills, teamwork, and a love for the outdoors while competing on iconic Sonoran Desert trails.

🔗 Arizona Cycling 🏫 STMS School Site

1

Fill Out Registration

2

ACA Membership

3

4

Attend Practice

Show up to our weekly practices and get ready to race!

🪪 ACA Membership

Arizona Cycling Association (ACA) membership is required for all student athletes and volunteer coaches.

  • 💳 Membership Fee: $125 paid directly to ACA
  • 🆓 Try It Out: Free one-time registration available
  • 🤝 Financial Assistance: Available through ACA for those who need it
🔗 Join ACA 🆓 STMS Try It Out 🆓 CSMTB Try Out
🏁 Race Series & ACA Fees

Riders can race some, all, or none of the four series races held September–October. Student athletes who qualify may compete at the ACA State Championships in November.

Fees Paid Directly to ACA

  • 📦 Series Package: $200
  • 🏁 Individual Race: $60
  • ⚠️ Additional fee applies for day-of registration
