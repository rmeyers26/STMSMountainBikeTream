📋 Before You Register

Students must attend Sonoran Trails Middle School (STMS) or be homeschooled within our team area to register with the STMS Mountain Bike Team and associated Cactus Shadows Mountain Bike Team. A parent or guardian must complete this form. All riders also need to register on the ACA platform.

📱 TeamSnap Communications

After ACA registration and team sign-up have been completed, student athletes and volunteer coaches will be added to our TeamSnap group. This is where all team communication takes place and emergency contact information is stored.

Please ensure that the information profile for your rider is complete and kept up to date. If you are having trouble with TeamSnap, contact Coach Jason to get help making updates.

🗓️ Practices & Racing

Team practices are in the evening Tuesday, Thursday and in the morning Saturday. Attending practices is flexible, as schoolwork always comes first. Racing is optional and consists of 4 regular season races and a 5th Championship race, for those that qualify. These are held around the beautiful, diverse state of Arizona.
