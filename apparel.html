📏 Kit Sizing Guide

Jersey & bib kits run slim. Measure chest (inches) and compare below:

Size Chest (in) Height (in)
Youth XS 24–26 48–52
Youth S 26–28 52–56
Youth M 28–30 56–60
Youth L 30–32 60–64
Adult XS 32–34 64–66
Adult S 34–36 66–68
Adult M 36–38 68–70
Adult L 38–40 70–72
Adult XL 40–43 72–74
Adult XXL 43–46 74+

⚠️ When in doubt, size up — kit sizing runs slim.
