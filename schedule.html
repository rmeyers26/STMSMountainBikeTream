ℹ️ Preseason Note

The preseason runs April 1 – May 31. The maximum number of preseason activities teams may conduct has been increased from 6 to 8. The regular season begins June 1.

🏔️ North Conference

We compete as part of the North Conference in the Arizona Cycling Association (ACA) middle school league.

📍 Location Note

Race venues span Northern and Central Arizona. Plan to arrive at least 60 minutes before your start time for check-in and warm-up.

🏕️ Travel & Lodging Tip

Depending on the race start time for your Rider(s), you may want to travel the night before or stay the night after. This is not required and is up to each individual family. Many families even choose to group camp.

🔢 Race Number Plates

⚠️ Important – Number Plate Care & Return

All student-athletes will receive a race number plate and riders will use the same plate for the entire race series. Number plates have chip timing transponders on the backside — exercise care to avoid damage.

Please remove the number plate from the bike and return it to the head coach before loading for transport to prevent damage or loss.

Replacement number plates will be available at the registration tent for $25.

🚵 Pre-Ride

📋 Course Pre-Ride Hours

The course will be open for pre-ride to all registered student-athletes and licensed coaches on the following schedule:

  • Friday, 12:00 p.m. – sunset — Open to all registered student-athletes and licensed coaches
  • Saturday, ~3:00 p.m. – sunset — Open following the conclusion of racing
⚠️ Friday 3:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. — Restricted Window

From 3:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. on Friday, the course is open to the following groups only:

  • High school female student-athletes
  • Middle school student-athletes (female and male)
  • Their licensed coaches

High school boys are not allowed on the course during this period.

🪖 Required Race-Day Gear

🪖 Helmet

CPSC, ASTM, or EN-1078 certified mountain bike helmet required. No road helmets.

🧤 Gloves

Full-finger gloves required for all race categories to protect hands during falls.

🚲 Bike Check

Working brakes, no loose parts. Bikes will be inspected at check-in. Bring your own tools.
