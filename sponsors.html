⭐ Our Sponsors

Our generous sponsors make STMS Mountain Bike possible. Thank you for supporting student athletes!

Become a Sponsor

Why Sponsor?

Community sponsorship helps us keep low individual costs for families who want to get their kids involved in mountain biking. It is vital that we keep our team as accessible as possible so our local Mountain Biking Community continues to thrive!

How it helps

Your support helps us buy and maintain important team equipment as well as pay for gas when hauling our team trailer to races. Thanks to our sponsors we were able to purchase a pop-up to keep our kids cool and safe on race days! Thank you sponsors!

The STMS Mountain Bike Team Sonoran Trails Middle School and associated with Cactus Shadows Mountain Bike Team races under the Arizona Cycling Association (ACA), the governing body for interscholastic mountain biking in Arizona. ACA organizes our race season, manages athlete registration, and partners with brands to provide exclusive member discounts.
