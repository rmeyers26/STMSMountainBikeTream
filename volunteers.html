Why Volunteer?

Your time makes race day happen for every rider

🏁

Race Day Support

Course marshals, check-in helpers, timing crew — your role keeps races running safely and smoothly.

🥪

Rider Fueling

Help manage the food and water stations to keep our riders energized through every lap.

🛠️

Mechanical Support

Mechanically inclined? Help riders with quick fixes and bike checks before the start line.

📸

Photography

Capture the action! Race photos are treasured by riders and families for years to come.

🎽

Practice Helpers

Assist coaches during practice sessions. No cycling experience needed — enthusiasm required!

📣

Announcer / Cheerleader

Keep the energy high! Help announce results and cheer on all the riders as they cross the finish line.
